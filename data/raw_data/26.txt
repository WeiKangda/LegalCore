MASTER OPERATION AND MAINTENANCE AGREEMENT

This MASTER OPERATION AND MAINTENANCE AGREEMENT (this "Agreement"),  dated  as of  April 13, 2012 , between BLOOM ENERGY CORPORATION, a Delaware corporation (" BE  " or, in its capacity as operator hereunder, "Operator"), and DIAMOND STATE GENERATION PARTNERS, LLC, a Delaware limited liability company ("Owner") (each, a "Party", and together, the "Parties"),  {E11 covers}  (i) the Portfolio of on-site solid oxide fuel cell power generating systems capable of being  {E12 powered}  by renewable fuels,  having  an aggregate Nameplate Capacity of up to 30 MW (each a "Bloom System", and together the "Bloom Systems") and (ii) the BOF  {E13 installed}  by BE pursuant to the MESPA, in each case to the extent set forth herein. WHEREAS, Owner is a company  {E14 formed}  at the direction of BE for the purpose of  purchasing  and  owning  Bloom Systems for the generation of electricity and sale of electricity and capacity  {E15 generated}  by the Bloom Systems into the PJM Grid;

WHEREAS, the customer base of Delmarva Power & Light Company ("DPL"), an investor  {E16 owned}  utility company  {E17 regulated}  by the Delaware Public Service Commission ("DPSC"), will be subject to a charge to be  {E18 collected}  on behalf of Owner by DPL under the REPS Act and the Tariffs, and DPL has  {E19 agreed}  to  {E20 provide}  natural gas service and to  {E21 serve}  as the collection and disbursement agent of Owner pursuant to the Tariffs and the DPL Agreements;

WHEREAS in  2011 , Owner  {E22 purchased}  from Operator pursuant to the  December 30  Bill of Sale certain Bloom Systems and other parts and equipment to be  {E23 incorporated}  into the Bloom Systems, and Owner presently  {E24 owns}  such Bloom Systems and other parts and equipment;

WHEREAS, Operator has  {E25 entered}  into that certain Master Energy Server Purchase Agreement  {E26 dated}  as of the date hereof (the "MESPA") with Owner, under the terms of which Owner will  {E27 purchase}  additional Bloom Systems and the BOF from BE in order for Owner to  {E28 provide}  electricity and capacity  {E29 generated}  by the Bloom Systems into the PJM Grid;

WHEREAS, pursuant to REPS Act Section 352(16), BE will be a "Qualified Fuel Cell Provider" ("QFCP"), and pursuant to the QFCP-RC Tariff, Owner will be a "QFCP Generator" ("QFCP Generator"), and pursuant to REPS Act Section 352(17) the Facilities shall  {E30 constitute}  a "Qualified Fuel Cell Provider Project" ("Qualified Fuel Cell Provider Project"); and

WHEREAS, Operator has  {E31 agreed}  to  {E32 provide}  certain operation and maintenance services to Owner subject to the conditions of this Agreement. Source: BLOOM ENERGY CORP, DRS/A (on S-1),  3/21/2018
